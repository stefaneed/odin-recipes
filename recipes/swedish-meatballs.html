Swedish Meatballs

Description

Meatballs cooked in a savory cream sauce that are usually paired with pasta or mashed potatoes.

Ingredients:

  • 2lbs premade meatballs (can also be made from scratch)
  • 2 Tbsp olive oil
  • 1/4 C. butter
  • 2 Tbsp flour
  • 2 C. beef broth
  • 1 Tbsp garlic (minced)
  • 1 C. heavy cream
  • 1 tsp Worcestershire sauce
  • salt (to taste)
  • pepper (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Bake meatballs in oven according to directions on the package.
  2. In a pan melt butter on medium heat until bubbly then add flour and whisk until light brown.
  3. Add garlic and cook for additional 1-2 minutes.
  4. Add beef broth slowly while whisking until everthing is mixed together.
  5. Slowly add in heavy cream while whisking until sauce starts to thicken.
  6. Add Worcestershire sauce, salt, and pepper.
  7. Combine cooked meatballs with cream sauce.
  8. Optional: enjoy your meatballs on top of some egg noodles or mashed potatoes.
