Fettuccine Alfredo

Description

An Italian pasta dish consisting of fettuccine, butter, parmesan cheese, cream, and seasonings.

Ingredients:

  • fettuccine pasta
  • 4 Tbsp butter
  • 2 Tbsp flour
  • 2 C. cream
  • 1 C. parmesan cheese
  • 1 Tbsp garlic
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to box directions, then set aside.
  2. Melt butter on medium heat until it starts to bubble then add flour, whisk for a minute or two until the mixture turns a light brown color.
  3. Add in minced garlic and cook for an additional minute.
  4. Slowly add in cream while whisking and continue until the sauce begins to thicken.
  5. A little at a time add in shredded parmesan and whisk into the sauce.
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Add cooked fettuccine pasta to sauce, mix together, and enjoy!
